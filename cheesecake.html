  • Cheesecake
I've been helping in the kitchen since I was young, mixing meatloaf and icing cookies. Despite being a finicky eater, I love cooking and baking. Fastforward to the fall of 2006, a coworker was celebrating her birthday. I wanted to make her a cake, but not something from a box. I wanted a challenge. So I set out to make a delicious cheesecake. The first one I made was a chocolate truffle. I soon became known as the cheesecake guy. Now I've crafted over sixty flavors. Each flavor brings a new challenge. I've also found a love for mini-cheesecake pans. I've brought them to picnics, D&D nights, work potlucks and special occasions. While I love baking, I love it as a hobby only.
